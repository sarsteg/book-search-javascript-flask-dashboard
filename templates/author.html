About the author....

Stephen King as we know has been dubbed the "King of Horror", and has been writing for over 40 years. During his career he has developed a strong work ethic by writing everyday which allowed him to accomplish writing 50 novels and 200 short stories. Here is a quote from him talking about his journey to success:


“I like to get ten pages a day, which amounts to 2,000 words. That’s 180,000 words over a three-month span, a goodish length for a book — something in which the reader can get happily lost, if the tale is done well and stays fresh.”

― Stephen King, On Writing: A Memoir of the Craft


It’s hard to imagine writing a book every 3 months. Thanks to his hard work and dedication we have novels and short stories we all know and love today.
